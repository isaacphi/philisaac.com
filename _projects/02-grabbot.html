My fascination with user interfaces might have begun in 2014 when I entered a two day intensive robotics hackathon. My team built a small robotic claw that was controlled entirely via gesture recognition.
Photo of the robot
The project combined a servo motor controlled robot, custom 3D printed parts, an arduino, and a leap motion controller for gesture recognition.
Grab bot in action
We ended up winning first place in technical innovation at the hackathon: article.
