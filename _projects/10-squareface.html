Squareface is my first full video game. It is currently in development but a simple demo is online below (it may require up to a minute to load, depending on your connection).
> PLAY <
Squareface is a simple 2D platformer that will be set in a surreal downtown toronto. As time passes, everyday objects transform into puzzles, platforms, and enemies. Get out before you completely lose your mind.
It is built in C# using Unity. I am working with Mason Barnes-Crouse on this project. Some early sprite sheets are shown below.
